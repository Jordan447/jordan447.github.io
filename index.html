How to Use?

  1. Download the image via the download button to your computer.
  2. Go to Imgur or postimages and upload the downloaded image.
  3. Go to any 24/7 and buy notes.
  4. Use /myinvex for the id, then use /note [id of the blank note item in your inventory] amount name LSSD Written Warning F. Lastname
  5. Use /myinvex for the id, then use /note [id of the new note item in your inventory] amount content URL from Imgur/postimages ending with .png
  6. [!] If you keep the name and content the same of every note they can be stacked.
  7. Use /giveitem to give out your cards to other people.
